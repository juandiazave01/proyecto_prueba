About this project

Written from the floor up

Reliability & Controls Insight is written by Juan Díaz, an industrial engineer with two decades across maintenance, quality, and industrial controls — including hands-on PLC programming, SCADA integration, and the transition from reactive maintenance into structured reliability engineering. This site collects what that work has actually taught, written for technicians, engineers, and plant leaders who'd rather read something practical than theoretical.
