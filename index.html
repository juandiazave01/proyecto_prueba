2021–present #CTL-11

Maintenance & Controls Engineer, Specialty Nonwovens Manufacturing

Implemented a new CMMS from scratch — equipment hierarchy, PM schedules, desktop and mobile rollout. Programs and commissions Siemens S120/G120 drives on TIA Portal, partners with the maintenance manager on annual reliability goals, and built a real-time Node-RED dashboard plus a Python-based carbon footprint and energy-consumption tracking project (ISO 50001).

2025–now #REL-12

Transition into Reliability Engineering

Rebuilding failure data into structured reliability analysis — Weibull models, FMEA libraries, RPN scoring — and pairing it with AI tooling that helps technicians diagnose faster instead of guessing.
